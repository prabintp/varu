Caring for your health. Since 1972

The roots of Shastha group dates back to 1972. The year when Dr. Vijayan Peroth, the elder son of Appukkuttan Guruswamikal chose to give an formal dimension to a medical legacy that beckoned him through 4 generations. Peroth family has been practicing medicine for ages. The generations beforewere proficient in Kalari, Astrology, toxicology, Bhaishajya Kalpana, and related disciplines. Dr. Vijayan was the first in the family to obtain a formal degree in medicine and eventually spear headed the formal evolution of Shastha Ayurvedics.
